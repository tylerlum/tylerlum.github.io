Tyler Lum

Creating intelligent and adaptable AI with deep reinforcement learning

Hi, I'm Tyler Lum and I am an Engineering Physics student in my last year of study at the University of British Columbia. I'm very interested in building intelligent systems that can be deployed in complex and high-stakes environments, including autonomous vehicles, medical decision-making, and robotics. In particular, I would like to improve the data efficiency, out-of-distribution performance, and explainability of AI. Through AI research, I hope to discover the underlying principles behind curious and adaptable behaviour, which will allow for robots to build the intelligence needed to create value for people in their everyday lives.
